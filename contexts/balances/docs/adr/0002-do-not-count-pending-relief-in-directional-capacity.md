# Do Not Count Pending Relief in Directional Capacity

For capacity, each Pending Transaction contributes one Net Account Effect per Account. Decrease Capacity subtracts the aggregate magnitudes of decreasing effects and Reserve Amount effects, while Increase Capacity subtracts the aggregate magnitudes of increasing effects. Opposing Postings net only inside their Transaction; effects from independently resolvable Transactions never offset, so movement away from a bound releases capacity only after posting.
