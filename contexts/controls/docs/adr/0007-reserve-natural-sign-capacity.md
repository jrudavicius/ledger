# Reserve Natural-Sign Capacity

A Reserve Amount reduces a Ledger Account's Decrease Capacity independently of whether that Account is Debit-normal or Credit-normal. It retains its full outstanding amount when it exceeds current capacity and continues to encumber downward capacity created by future natural-sign balance increases until adjusted or ended; it does not affect Increase Capacity under a Balance Ceiling. This avoids the false assumption that future funds always arrive through credits without conflating spending capacity with receivable exposure.
