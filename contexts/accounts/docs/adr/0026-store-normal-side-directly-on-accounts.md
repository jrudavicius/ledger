# Store Normal Side Directly on Accounts

Each Account stores exactly one immutable Normal Side, Debit or Credit, rather than an Asset, Liability, Equity, Revenue, or Expense Account Classification. Posted Balance and balance-consuming direction derive directly from Normal Side, while Account Role remains independent. Products and reporting taxonomies remain outside the accounting contexts and select the Normal Side when creating Accounts, keeping accounting domain-neutral without forcing product or reporting categories into the Accounts model.
