# Stage Account Opening Through Pending and Opening

An Account is created in Pending and progresses through Pending, Opening, Open, Closing, and terminal Closed. Pending and Opening reject ordinary Transactions, while Opening-to-Open requires the Account Opening Gate and can be denied by an Active Account-targeted Control; ending that Control removes one denial but never opens the Account automatically.

This gives pre-open work a stable Account identity that Controls can target without turning a compliance finding into an Account State. The abandonment path for a Pending or Opening Account that will never open remains a separate lifecycle decision.
